River Bucks Gift Certificates

Give River Bucks gift certificates to your friends and family and they can use them to pay for WPWA events, new memberships, and things from the WPWA store. You can order River Bucks in any dollar amount and have them emailed to you or emailed directly to the recipient.

Shipping Rates on Order Totals:

  • Up to $15: $5 shipping
  • Up to $30: $8 shipping
  • Up to $50: $12 shipping
  • Orders over $50 receive free shipping.

Please Note:

In figuring the order total for shipping, tax is not including nor are memberships, donations, event registrations or River Bucks gift certificate purchases. No shipping is charge on these items.

For orders shipping to Rhode Island addresses or picked up at the WPWA office 7% sales tax is added to the order as required by law. This does not apply to clothing, donations, event registrations or River Bucks gift certificate purchases. If any of the items on the order are taxable then the shipping is also taxable, per Rhode Island law.
