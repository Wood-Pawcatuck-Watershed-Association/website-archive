Monday Painting Group (MPG)

A group of painters flocked to WPWA's campus on Monday, August 22nd for a lovely day on the Wood River. The informal gathering of painters meet on Mondays at various locations throughout the state. Hailing from Smithfield to Warwick to Hope Valley, the group is organized by local artist Susan Shaw. For more information on the arts in this area visit http://artcom.me/hoparts/ to find out when this year's studio trail will take place.
