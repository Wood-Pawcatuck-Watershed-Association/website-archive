Opening Day of Trout Season 2011

On April 9th, 2011, WPWA watched as many people gathered at the Barberville Dam to throw out the first casts of the trout fishing season. Though it was a chilly morning, the sun broke through and it was a beautiful day. Special Thanks to Green Mountain Coffee Roasters for donating coffee for the event; which was served free of charge to anyone who had a reusable mug!
