Thank you for Your Interest in Becoming a WPWA Volunteer!

Without the assistance of volunteers we could not do what we do! You don't need to have lots of free time to volunteer; even a few hours helping with a volunteer project on one weekend makes a big difference.

By filling out the form below you are not committing to volunteering you are just signing up so that we can let you know when volunteer opportunities arise. You do not need to provide all of the information below, but it is very helpful to us if you can provide an email address so that we can efficiently tell you about upcoming volunteer opportunities, and a phone number is a great back-up in case we have problems with your email address. If you have any questions please contact the WPWA Office at [email protected], (401) 539-9017.
