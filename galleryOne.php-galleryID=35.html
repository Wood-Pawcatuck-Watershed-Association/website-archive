2015 Children's Programs

This spring and summer we've taken dozens of children kayaking, canoeing, hiking, and netting macroinvertebrates, and the kids have had a blast. What a treat for WPWA's staff and dedicated volunteers to get these kids out on the water and into nature. It's amazing to watch kids get into a kayak for the first time in their lives. Sometimes they are a bit nervous starting out, but then they quickly get more comfortable and end up confident and having a great time. It's an experience that we hope will help foster a love of nature that lasts a lifetime.
