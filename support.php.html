Support the Cause:

Membership is open to anyone who is interested in the protection of the Pawcatuck Watershed's valuable natural resources. Membership year is from June to June. Membership categories are:

  • Individual $35.00
  • Family $50.00
  • Contributor $75.00
  • Corporate/Supporter $100.00
  • Sponsor $250.00
  • Patron $500.00
  • Benefactor $1000.00

Benefits of membership include free parking and river access at our Barberville headquarters, subscription to our quarterly newsletter Watershed, membership decal, discounts on WPWA programs and publications, and the satisfaction of helping the protect this important natural resource. Membership dues are tax deductible within the limits of the law. To become a member, simply click here or contact the office at (401) 539-9017 or [email protected], or send a check or money order to: WPWA, 203 Arcadia Road, Hope Valley, RI 02832-3230.
