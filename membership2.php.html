Please Join the Wood-Pawcatuck Watershed Association

or renew if you are already a member!

Membership is open to anyone who is interested in the protection of the Wood-Pawcatuck Watershed’s valuable natural resources.

To join or renew online, please fill out the form below and then click the Continue button to review your information. You will then be able to add the membership to your “shopping cart” to pay for your membership. Our online system uses PayPal to process payments but you do NOT need to have a PayPal account to use it. You can pay with most major credit cards.

If you prefer to join by mail please fill out this printable membership form (PDF format) and mail it to WPWA, 203 Arcadia Rd. Hope Valley, RI 02832 with your check or credit card number.

If you have any questions please contact the WPWA Office at [email protected], (401) 539-9017.

Our membership year is from June 1 to May 31.

Your base Individual Membership dues ($35) or base Household Membership dues ($50)
may be eligible for a tax deduction to a 501-c-3 charitable organization.

In addition to the satisfaction of helping to protect the watershed, membership benefits include (please choose one): Individual Household*
Dragonfly: Use of WPWA facilities and boats for a fee (subject to availability), members-only parking at WPWA’s Barberville headquarters, e-newsletter Watershed, membership decal, member discounts on all WPWA programs $35 $50
Trout (BEST value): All Dragonfly benefits, PLUS unlimited free boat and gear use for member and a guest (subject to availability), free copies of Wood- Pawcatuck River Guide and Walks in the Watershed $100 $175
Otter: All Trout benefits, PLUS free programs for member (subject to availability. Must transport WPWA boat if using it for an offsite program) $150 $250
Osprey: All Otter benefits, PLUS one full day facility use (subject to availability) $300 $400
Eagle: All Eagle benefits, PLUS unlimited facility use (subject to availability) $750 $1,000

* A HOUSEHOLD membership applies to each person living in your home.
