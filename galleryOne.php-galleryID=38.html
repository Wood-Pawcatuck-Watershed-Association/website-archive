2015 Volunteer Appreciation Day

On 10/12/15, WPWA was happy to thank our dedicated and wonderful volunteers with a lovely paddle/hike down the Wood River from Rt. 165 to the WPWA campus at Barberville Dam, followed by a BBQ. There wasn't much water in some places, requiring us to get out and pull our boats a "few" times. We heard "this should be the last time" a bunch of times! But it was a spectacular fall day, the leaves were gorgeous, and everyone had a great time. We can't tell our volunteers enough how much we appreciate you--thank you!!!
